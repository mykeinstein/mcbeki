About Us

Everything you need to know about CyBeratung Limited

CyBeratung Ltd

The New lead Brand-ICT Firm

CyBeratung is an audio-visual branding, Cybersecurity and ICT firm that prides in product and content creation for organisations, bridging the gap between end users and products or services. Over time, we have realized that many brands and businesses unconsciously do not demand excellence and as a result, settle for lesser standards than can be attained, thus struggling to effectively communicate their products and services. At CyBeratung, our creative process involves synergising with our clients, getting into their mental space and reproducing their wants and requirements accurately to the last detail. We achieve this because the company is founded on the bedrock of creative excellence. Our goal is to leave a lasting legacy to end users on brand perception and product creation in media and the technology space. In the saturated world of branding and advertising where brands and businesses lack awareness, assisting businesses rise above the noise is our specialty. CyBeratung has served Industry frontiers like LAPO Microfinance Bank, DHL, Grand Cereals (UACN subsidiary) & others. We possess a creative team that specializes in building start-up businesses and improving existing ones. Get in touch with us today; we are your potential partners.
